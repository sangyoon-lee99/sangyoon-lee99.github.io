Sangyoon Lee

I’m a second-year M.S.-Ph.D. student in GSAI (Graduate School of Artificial Intelligence) at POSTECH Efficient Learning Lab (EffL), advised by Prof. Jaeho Lee.

My research interests lie in accelerating neural network training and understanding optimization dynamics through the lens of spectral and simplicity biases. I have mainly focused on neural fields, exploring methods to accelerate and improve training efficiency.
